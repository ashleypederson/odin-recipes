Brocolli Cheddar Soup

A copycat recipe of Panera's famous Broccoli Cheddar Soup! Using only a handful of ingredients, you can enjoy this delicious meal in the comfort of your own home any time. This soup is especially good paried with your favorite crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 2 cups chicken stock or broth
  • 3 cups broccoli florets, chopped into bite size pieces
  • 1 cup carrot, julienned or "matchstick"
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in pot or dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until translucent. Sprinkle the flour over the onions, cooking and stirring for 1-2 minutes or until the mixture begins to golden.
  2. Slowly whisk in the chicken stock, stirring constantly and allowing mixture to thicken. Add half & half and continue to stir.
  3. Add the broccoli and carrots. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the broccoli and carrots are tender.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste, as well as the optional nutmeg to bring out extra flavor. For a smoother soup, remove 1-2 cups of soup and blend until smooth before returning to the soup.
  5. Add the cheese to the soup while still on low heat, stirring to melt. Note: the soup can become grainy if the heat is too high.
  6. Serve soup with crusty bread.
